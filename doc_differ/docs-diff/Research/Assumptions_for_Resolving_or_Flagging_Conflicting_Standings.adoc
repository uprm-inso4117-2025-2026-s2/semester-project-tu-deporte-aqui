== [.inserted]#1. Context#

[.inserted]#This document defines assumptions for handling conflicting standings data by either:#

* [.inserted]#Resolving differences automatically#
* [.inserted]#Flagging discrepancies for review or user awareness#

---

== [.inserted]#2. Common Conflicting Scenarios#

=== [.inserted]#2.1 Points or Record Totals Do Not Match#

* [.inserted]#One data feed updates earlier than others (latency)#
* [.inserted]#Matches may be amended due to:#
- [.inserted]#Forfeits#
- [.inserted]#Administrative decisions#

---

=== [.inserted]#2.2 Ranking Order Differs (Same Points)#

* [.inserted]#Different sources may apply *different tiebreaker rules*#
* [.inserted]#Tiebreakers may include:#
- [.inserted]#Goal difference#
- [.inserted]#Goals scored#
- [.inserted]#Head-to-head#
- [.inserted]#Disciplinary points#

[.inserted]#IMPORTANT:#

[.inserted]#The order of these rules is *competition-specific*#

---

=== [.inserted]#2.3 Live vs Official Standings#

* [.inserted]#Live standings use *provisional data*#
* [.inserted]#Official standings are updated after league confirmation#

Observation::

[.inserted]#Platforms reconcile live data with official results after matches end#

---

=== [.inserted]#2.4 Partial Data or Missing Matches#

* [.inserted]#A provider may not have ingested all matches#
* [.inserted]#Postponed matches may be:#
- [.inserted]#Incorrectly marked as played#
- [.inserted]#Omitted entirely#

---

=== [.inserted]#2.5 Identity Mapping Issues#

* [.inserted]#Team naming inconsistencies:#
- [.inserted]#"Man United" vs "Manchester United"#
* [.inserted]#League phase changes#
* [.inserted]#Team relocation#

Result::

[.inserted]#Data may be associated with incorrect entities#

---

== [.inserted]#3. Data Validation Strategies (Industry Practices)#

=== [.inserted]#3.1 Source Hierarchy#

* [.inserted]#Official > Provider > Community#

[.inserted]#Official sources are treated as the *final authority*#

Example::

[.inserted]#Platforms update results after official league corrections#

---

=== [.inserted]#3.2 Rule-Based Validation Checks#

[.inserted]#Automated checks include:#

* [.inserted]#Points consistency  #
[.inserted]#  (e.g., points = 3W + 1D in soccer)#

* [.inserted]#Matches played consistency  #
[.inserted]#  (Played = W + D + L)#

* [.inserted]#Goal difference validation  #
[.inserted]#  (GD = goals for - goals against)#

* [.inserted]#Ranking consistency  #
[.inserted]#  (Higher points → higher rank unless rules differ)#

---

=== [.inserted]#3.3 Event Log (Event Sourcing)#

* [.inserted]#Store raw match events and updates#
* [.inserted]#Rebuild standings deterministically#

Benefits::

* [.inserted]#Traceability#
* [.inserted]#Auditability#
* [.inserted]#Ability to replay history#

---

=== [.inserted]#3.4 Dual-View Publishing#

* [.inserted]#Live (unofficial, fast, may change)#
* [.inserted]#Official (slower, stable)#

---

== [.inserted]#4. Assumptions and Resolution Rules#

=== [.inserted]#Assumption 1 – Competition Rules Define Truth#

* [.inserted]#Store competition-specific tiebreaker rules#
* [.inserted]#Do NOT rely on provider ranking#

Implementation::

* [.inserted]#Compute ranking locally using official rules#

---

=== [.inserted]#Assumption 2 – Official Sources Override Live#

[.inserted]#If conflict occurs:#

* [.inserted]#Display both versions#
* [.inserted]#Prioritize official source#

UI Behavior::

* [.inserted]#Official = primary#
* [.inserted]#Provider/live = secondary#
* [.inserted]#Include timestamps and labels#

---

=== [.inserted]#Assumption 3 – Resolve vs Flag Based on Severity#

==== [.inserted]#Auto-Resolve (No User Impact)#

* [.inserted]#Differences caused by latency#
* [.inserted]#Differences within correction window after game end#

---

==== [.inserted]#Flag for Review (User Warning)#

* [.inserted]#Point totals differ after official finalization#
* [.inserted]#Ranking differs due to unclear tiebreak rules#

---

==== [.inserted]#Hard Block (Do Not Publish as Official)#

* [.inserted]#Invalid data invariants:#

- [.inserted]#Played ≠ W + D + L#
- [.inserted]#Goal difference mismatch#
- [.inserted]#Impossible point values#
- [.inserted]#Missing teams#

---

=== [.inserted]#Assumption 4 – Always Explain Differences#

[.inserted]#When conflicts are detected, display:#

* [.inserted]#Reason for discrepancy:#
- [.inserted]#Tiebreaker rule#
- [.inserted]#Feed delay#
- [.inserted]#Stat correction#

* [.inserted]#Metadata:#
- [.inserted]#Last updated time#
- [.inserted]#Source#

---

== [.inserted]#5. Transparency Principle#

[.inserted]#The system must clearly communicate:#

* [.inserted]#Why standings differ#
* [.inserted]#Which source is prioritized#
* [.inserted]#When data was last updated#

[.inserted]#This aligns with industry practices for:#

* [.inserted]#Trust#
