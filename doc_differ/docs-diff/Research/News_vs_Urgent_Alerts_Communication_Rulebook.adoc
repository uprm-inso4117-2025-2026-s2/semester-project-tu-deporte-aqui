[.inserted]#Project: Tu Deporte Aquí#

== [.inserted]#Objective#

[.inserted]#Set clear rules and user-facing wording for when Tu Deporte Aquí posts:#

* [.inserted]#Regular sports news#
* [.inserted]#Urgent alerts#

[.inserted]#and how to communicate:#

* [.inserted]#Freshness#
* [.inserted]#Uncertainty#

[.inserted]#in a calm and transparent way.#

---

== [.inserted]#1. Decision Checklist: News vs Urgent Alert#

=== [.inserted]#Post as NEWS (routine) when…#

* [.inserted]#It is a normal update (preview, recap, highlights, quotes, standings)#
* [.inserted]#Timing is not critical#
* [.inserted]#Information is stable or confirmed#
* [.inserted]#No immediate user action is required#

---

=== [.inserted]#Post as URGENT ALERT when…#

* [.inserted]#Immediate impact:#
* [.inserted]#Postponement or suspension#
* [.inserted]#Venue change#
* [.inserted]#Start time change#
* [.inserted]#Weather disruption#
* [.inserted]#High impact + time-sensitive:#
* [.inserted]#Playoff implications#
* [.inserted]#Official corrections affecting current games#
* [.inserted]#User action required:#
* [.inserted]#Travel changes#
* [.inserted]#Game delays#
* [.inserted]#Stream changes#
* [.inserted]#Rapidly evolving situations:#
* [.inserted]#"Under review"#
* [.inserted]#"Developing"#
* [.inserted]#"Awaiting confirmation"#

---

=== [.inserted]#Reliability-First Rule#

[.inserted]#If information is urgent but unverified:#

* [.inserted]#It may still be posted#
* [.inserted]#Must be labeled clearly as:#
* [.inserted]#Unverified#
* [.inserted]#Developing#
* [.inserted]#Tone must remain neutral#
* [.inserted]#Clearly distinguish:#
* [.inserted]#What is known#
* [.inserted]#What is not confirmed#

== [.inserted]#3. Definitions#

=== [.inserted]#Unverified#

* [.inserted]#Only one source or non-official source#
* [.inserted]#Not confirmed by official or multiple reliable sources#

---

=== [.inserted]#Delayed#

* [.inserted]#Official data not yet available#
* [.inserted]#Feeds are lagging or unavailable#

---

=== [.inserted]#Conflicting#

* [.inserted]#Multiple credible sources disagree#
* [.inserted]#Key data differs:#
* [.inserted]#Score#
* [.inserted]#Status#
* [.inserted]#Timing#

== [.inserted]#4. User-Facing Message Templates#

[.inserted]#Brackets indicate dynamic system values.#

---

=== [.inserted]#A) Routine News#

==== [.inserted]#Recap Posted#

----
[.inserted]#Final: [TEAM A] [A]–[B] [TEAM B]. Recap and key plays are live.#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Standings Update#

----
[.inserted]#Standings updated at [TIME]. [TEAM] is now [POSITION] in [LEAGUE].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Player Status#

----
[PLAYER] status: Confirmed [IN/OUT] for today.
----

---

=== [.inserted]#B) Urgent Alerts (Confirmed)#

==== [.inserted]#OFFICIAL Postponement#

----
[.inserted]#OFFICIAL: [GAME] is postponed. Reason: [REASON]. Next update: [EXPECTED TIME].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Time Change#

----
[.inserted]#Update: [GAME] new start time is [NEW TIME]. Previous: [OLD TIME].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Discipline#

----
[.inserted]#OFFICIAL: [PLAYER/TEAM] disciplinary decision posted. Summary: [SHORT SUMMARY].#
----

---

=== [.inserted]#C) Uncertainty / Degraded States#

==== [.inserted]#Developing (Unverified)#

----
[.inserted]#Developing: Reports say [CLAIM]. Not yet confirmed by official sources. Last checked: [TIME].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Conflicting Reports#

----
[.inserted]#Conflicting reports:#
[.inserted]#• Source A: [CLAIM A]#
[.inserted]#• Source B: [CLAIM B]#
[.inserted]#Waiting for official confirmation. Next check: [TIME].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Delayed Data#

----
[.inserted]#Standings are delayed (source update pending). Showing last confirmed standings from [TIME].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Correction#

----
[.inserted]#Correction: Previously [OLD INFO]. Confirmed now: [NEW INFO]. Updated at [TIME].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Degraded Mode#

----
[.inserted]#Some updates may be slower than usual. We are tracking official posts and refreshing as feeds stabilize. Last update: [TIME].#
----

---

==== [.inserted]#Reassurance Message#

----
[.inserted]#This is moving fast. We will only mark it OFFICIAL once it is confirmed.#
----

== [.inserted]#6. Testing Plan (Review-Only)#

[cols="1,2", options="header"]
[.inserted]#|===#
[.inserted]#| Failure Scenario | Expected Behavior#

[.inserted]#| Conflicting score reports#
[.inserted]#| Mark as Conflicting; show both claims; do not label "Final"; include Last updated and Next check.#

[.inserted]#| Unverified breaking update#
[.inserted]#| Label as Unverified / Developing; maintain neutral tone; include Last checked.#

[.inserted]#| Delayed standings/stats#
[.inserted]#| Mark as Delayed; show last confirmed data; include timestamp.#

[.inserted]#| Source outage#
[.inserted]#| Enter Degraded mode; show cached data; indicate slower updates.#

[.inserted]#| Missing final status#
[.inserted]#| Keep Unconfirmed; do not mark Final; include Next check.#

[.inserted]#| Partial data#
[.inserted]#| Show available data; hide missing sections; label as Partial.#

[.inserted]#| Late correction#
[.inserted]#| Publish Correction; show old vs new; update timestamp.#

[.inserted]#| Load spike#
[.inserted]#| Throttle updates; prioritize core data; show Updating… and last confirmed state.#
[.inserted]#|===#
