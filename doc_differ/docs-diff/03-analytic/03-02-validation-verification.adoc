===== [.removed]#Purpose#

[.removed]#This section defines how the concepts, assumptions, and reliability strategies proposed for Tu Deporte Aquí will be evaluated during Milestone 1. Since this milestone focuses on documentation rather than implementation, validation emphasizes conceptual correctness, clarity, and alignment with project goals rather than executable testing.#

[.removed]#Validation ensures that the proposed system behavior addresses real-world conditions of local Puerto Rico sports data, including delayed updates, conflicting sources, and limited official APIs. Verification confirms that documentation artifacts are internally consistent and correctly structured.#

[.removed]#Validation during Milestone 1 focuses on confirming that the proposed concepts meet stakeholder expectations and realistic operating conditions.#
