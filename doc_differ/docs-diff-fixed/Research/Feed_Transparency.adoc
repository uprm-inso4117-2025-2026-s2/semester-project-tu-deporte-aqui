= Feed Transparency Feature: “Why am I seeing this?”
Author: Diego A. Rios Morales
Date: February 27, 2026

== Objective

The objective of this research is to design a transparency feature that explains to users why a specific news item appears in their feed.

== Problem

When users do not understand how their feed behaves, confusion increases.

A short “reason label” per story can:

* Reduce confusion
* Improve trust
* Allow users to decide whether they want to continue seeing similar content

== UX Principles

* *Non-intrusive*: Must be short and direct
* *Privacy*: Do not expose sensitive information
* *Actionable*: Must include options such as mute, follow, or manage preferences

== Explanation Categories (Proposed)

Each story should include:

* 1 primary reason (always visible)
* Up to 2 secondary reasons (shown in details view)

=== Following / Subscriptions

* Shown because you follow Team X
* Shown because you follow League Y
* From a source you follow

=== Location / Regional Relevance

* Trending in your region

NOTE: Use only broad region (no precise location)

=== Breaking / Urgent

* Breaking news
* Game-time update
* Injury update (sports-specific)

=== Recency / Recently Changed

* Recently updated
* Score updated

=== Popularity Signal

* Trending now
* Popular with fans of Team X

=== System / Product Rules

* Featured
* Pinned by admins
* Recommended for new users

== UI Placement Options

=== Option A (Recommended): Micro-label under headline

Place a one-line reason label under the story title, before time/source metadata.

==== Pros

* Always visible
* Builds trust passively

==== Cons

* Must remain short (one line maximum)

=== Option B: Info icon only

Show only an `(i)` icon near time/source; tapping reveals the reason.

==== Pros

* Minimal visual clutter

==== Cons

* Lower discoverability

== Interaction Behavior

=== Default Behavior

* Show primary reason label under headline (Option A)
* Include a small `(i)` icon next to the label for details

=== On Click / Tap

* Desktop: Open a popover panel

=== Details Panel Content (Example)

*Title:* Why you’re seeing this

*Reasons (max 3):*
* You follow Team X
* Trending in your region
* Recently updated

*Actions:*
* Manage feed preferences
* Mute Team X
* Hide this topic
* Learn how ranking works

== Copy and Privacy Rules

=== Guidelines

* Avoid precise tracking statements like:
** “Because you searched ____ yesterday”

* Prefer privacy-safe phrasing:
** “Based on your recent activity”

* Do not show exact location
** Use region-level language only

=== Priority Order for Primary Reason

1. Following
2. Breaking
3. Local / Trending
4. Recently updated
5. Similar interest

== Edge Cases

=== Cold Start (New Users)

* Recommended for you
* Popular right now

=== Old News Resurfacing

* Trending again
* Popular this week
