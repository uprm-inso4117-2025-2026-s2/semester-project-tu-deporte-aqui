==== 1.3.2

===== Synopsis

Tu Deporte Aquí addresses the problem of following local sports information in Puerto Rico when updates are spread across multiple sources and may differ in timing, detail, and reliability.

Scores, standings, schedules, and event updates are commonly published through league pages, social media accounts, sports media, reporters, and third-party platforms. Because of this, it can be difficult to determine what information is current, what is uncertain, and how much confidence can be placed in a given update.

The project focuses on local Puerto Rico sports. National and international coverage is outside its scope.

The system does not assume that all available information is complete or consistent. Updates may be delayed, partial, conflicting, or later corrected. For that reason, the project places attention on how information is presented, including its state, timing, and level of confirmation.

The goal is to improve how local sports information is organized and interpreted in situations where sources differ and conditions are imperfect.
