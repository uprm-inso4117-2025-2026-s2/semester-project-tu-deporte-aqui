
==== 1.3.2

===== Synopsis

Tu Deporte Aquí addresses the difficulty of following local sports information in Puerto Rico when relevant updates are distributed across many independent sources with different levels of detail, timing, and dependability.

People who follow local sports frequently encounter situations in which schedules, standings, scores, postponements, and live updates appear in different places and may not agree with one another. Information may be delayed, incomplete, corrected later, or communicated without sufficient context.

The project focuses specifically on local Puerto Rico sports environments, including leagues, school programs, and community-level sporting events. National and international coverage are outside the intended scope.

A central concern of the project is not only access to sports information, but also communication of the condition of that information. The project therefore treats uncertainty, delay, incompleteness, and conflicting reports as normal conditions that must be represented clearly instead of hidden.

The project aims to improve how local sports information is organized, interpreted, and consulted under conditions where reporting practices, update timing, and source dependability vary significantly between organizations and contributors.
